A free, open-source wrapper around [Wispr Flow](https://wispr.com) that removes its 6-minute recording limit and adds automatic note generation to [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md). **macOS only.**

Record a meeting, lecture, brainstorm, or paper discussion — when you stop, structured notes appear in your Obsidian vault. Meetings get action items. Lectures get study notes. Everything is organized into the folder you choose.

You keep the full transcript, and you control exactly how your notes are generated by editing simple prompt templates. Different types of recordings can produce completely different styles of notes.

## Getting Started

Two things you need first:

1. **[Wispr Flow](https://wispr.com)** — install it, do one test recording so it's set up
2. **A free Google AI account** — [grab an API key here](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey) (takes 30 seconds)

Then open Terminal and paste this:

```bash
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yashsmehta/wispr-unleashed/main/scripts/get.sh | bash
```

The installer walks you through everything — just follow the prompts.

## Usage

```bash
wispr "Weekly Standup"
```

That's it. Press `Ctrl+C` when the meeting ends. You'll pick a folder in your vault, and your notes show up in Obsidian.

The type of notes you get depends on where you save them:

- **Project folders** — meeting notes with action items
- **Talks / Lectures / Seminars** — structured study notes, no action items

## How It Works

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/diagram.png" alt="Recording pipeline" width="100%">
</p>

1. Starts Wispr Flow hands-free recording
2. Silently cycles every 5 minutes in the background (staying under Wispr's limit)
3. Stitches everything into one complete transcript
4. Generates structured notes and saves them to your Obsidian vault

Your raw transcript is always saved separately — you never lose the original.

> **Tip:** Grant Accessibility permission to your terminal app (System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility) and Wispr's transcription text won't accidentally paste into other apps while recording.

## Make It Your Own

### Note style

The prompts that shape your notes live in `~/wispr-unleashed/prompts/`. Open them in any text editor to change the style, structure, or level of detail. Since your raw transcript is always preserved, you can tweak a prompt and regenerate notes anytime.

### AI model

The default is Google's Gemini (free tier). You can switch to any provider — OpenAI, Anthropic, or others — by editing `~/wispr-unleashed/.env`:

```bash
# Google Gemini (default, free)
GOOGLE_API_KEY=your-key
LLM_MODEL=gemini/gemini-2.5-flash

# OpenAI
OPENAI_API_KEY=your-key
LLM_MODEL=gpt-4o-mini

# Anthropic
ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=your-key
LLM_MODEL=anthropic/claude-sonnet-4-20250514
```

### Obsidian vault

```bash
OBSIDIAN_VAULT=~/Desktop/Obsidian Vault    # where notes are saved
TRANSCRIPTS_DIR=$OBSIDIAN_VAULT/Transcripts # where raw transcripts go
```
